about

Our priorities: speed, quality, technology. Purpose: company development focusing on the needs and desires of customers. Vision: to become a team of experts in the field of metallurgy

Founded in 2017, F.M Welding OÜ offers metalworking and forging services.

Modern professional tools, highly qualified specialists, strict adherence to conditions and regulatory requirements are the criteria that ensure the success of welding work.
